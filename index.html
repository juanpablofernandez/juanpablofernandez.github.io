Confess

Confess allows you to anonymously send confessions to your Facebook friends. Your friends can react to your confession by swiping up and picking an emoji that best fits their feelings.

  • Date: February 2017
  • Tags: Swift, Facebook SDK, Firebase, Messaging, Authentication, Notifications

WhatsMyFare

WhatsMyFare is the simplest way to compare prices between Lyft and Uber. You'll be able to call the selected ride in a convenient way all within the app! It's quick, fast and convenient!

  • Download: WhatsMyFare
  • Date: October 2016
  • Tags: Swift, Google Maps SDK, Google Places API, Featured 3 times on the App Store category pages

SOS - Toontown

Easily access useful information about Toontown Rewritten to make progressing through the game a breeze. Find details on fishing, cogs, invasions, gags, toontasks, gardening and more!

  • Download: SOS - Toontown
  • Date: August 2016
  • Tags: Swift, Objective-C, 3000+ downloads, Featured on the App Store

SwiftyOnboard

SwiftyOnboard makes it easy to add onboarding to any iOS application. SwiftyOnboard handles all of the logic behind the pagination of views, which allows you to quickly add a highly customizable onboarding to your app, all in a lightweight framework.

  • Download: SwiftyOnboard
  • Date: March 2017
  • Tags: Swift, iOS, Framework, Open Sourced
